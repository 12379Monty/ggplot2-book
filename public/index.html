ggplot2 book

The ggplot book will be published by Springer in mid 2009. Until then, the latest draft version of the book will be available here. If you do download and read it, please send me your comments and criticism, so that I can improve it before it goes to press. This version of the book was produced on 19 November 2008. A crude change log is available if you want to see what's new.

ggplot2 is changing a little as I write the book, so don't be surprised if the code in the book doesn't work for you. I do my best to keep the two in sync, but the version on CRAN may be a little out of date.
